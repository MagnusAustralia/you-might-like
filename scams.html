Phishing

What is phishing?

Phishing is a form of scam. It is when emails are sent, usually using a bot, inviting you to click on a link. This link will often direct to you a form, asking for your personal data.

How to detect a Phishing Scam?

  1. The email is not directly addressed to you.
  2. The email will often say something bad will happen if you click on the link(such as your bank account may be blocked)
  3. The link will have the prefix "http" (which is unencrypted), rather than "https" (which is encrypted)
