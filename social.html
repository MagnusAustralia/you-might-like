Keeping Data Private

Internet scammers are always after your private data and information, such as your name, date of birth, address, email and more. In the wrong hands this data can be very dangerous to you and people you know, as this could lead to: phishing, scamming, grooming and stalking. Always remember to keep your accounts private, to prevent the leakage of this data. On public accounts, never post any personal information such as your address. Also, try not to post any pictures with your school uniform on, as this could alert potential stalkers to your location.

Understanding the Risks

Always remember that all internet activity, especially social media carries its risks. When you post something on social media, it is no longer your property. Never post anything that you would not feel comfortable if it was shared.

Staying Safe

There are many strategies you can use to stay safe online, and they can be as simple as checking the website or email is legitimate, setting up a firewall to block illicit websites, or update your antivirus to prevent malware (malicious software).
